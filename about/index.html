About me

Who am I ?

I always call myself inkdot online.

To understand this name, one should first imagine a white sheet stretching to infinity.

And then, somewhere on the plane, imagine an inkdot marking its own presence.

Now, how do you feel?

Unique? Outstanding? Or lonely?

They are all right answers,

all meanings I intended to convey in this name.

Travel

I like travelling.

I feel so small when I explore this world.

Every trip brings me new knowledge, new perspective, and reminds me more untouched experiences.

That’s why I stay humble and keep learning.

That’s what keep me alive.

The pursuit to new things and unseen view is the reason of my life.

Without it,

living feels like death.

Writing

I like writting.

I write stories, novels,

depicting the life I have seen but never lived.

They are like paths unchosen,

in this one-way road called life.

My writting style is intuition based.

I only write when words flow from the tip of my pen,

or pop up at the left of the cursor.

They are the negligible marks I left

in this incredibly colossal world.

Loneliness

I sometimes feel lonely.

Not because I have no friends.

But because of the fact that

we were born alone in this world.

We are alone as much as we are unique.

There will always be some fights that can only be fighten

by oneself.

And with a very small possibility, would one be lucky enough

to find a companion that can

really share similar fights.

Life is a journey of finding these companions.

Belonging

I feels I own nothing.

The people around me comes and leaves.

Their fate cannot be determined by me.

My friends and families don’t belong to me.

The objects, my properties.

They are lost and gained independent of my will.

My properties don’t belong to me.

My body, I lived with.

Still left on this world after my death.

My body doesn’t belong to me.

I own nothing.

Life

I feels that I’m always fighting.

Life is a fight.

A fight

against chaos,

against entropy,

against death.

Nature persists,

Life ends.

Life is not a part of nature.

It stands against nature.

It is unnatural.

Only when it dies, it becomes nature.

It will

dissolve,

disintegrate,

disappear,

just like the cosmos where life lives.

But before life ends,

it fights.

An eternal life is an eternal fight.

Not dead yet…
